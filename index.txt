          <h3>Process:</h3>
        <p><strong>Step 1:</strong> Fill the bottom of the steamer with 2 inches of water.<br><strong>Step 2:</strong>While steam is building up cut squash into 1" cubes.<br><strong>Step 3:</strong>Steam, covered with a tight fitting lid, for 7 minutes.Butternut Squash is done when it is tender (a fork caneasily pierce it), yet still firm enough to hold its shape.<br><strong>Step 4:</strong>Transfer to a bowl. For more flavor, toss Butternut Squash with the oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper while it is still hot. Research shows that fat-soluble vitamins and carotenoids found in foods, such as Winter Squash, may be better absorbed when consumed with fat-containing foods like extra virgin olive oil.</p>
